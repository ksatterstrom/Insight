Temozolomide (TMZ) (brand names Temodar, Temodal, and Temcad) is an oral chemotherapy drug used as a first-line treatment for glioblastoma. Based on your molecular profile, this utility will estimate how well you will respond to it (i.e., whether your survival will be above or below the median after treatment with the drug). Currently it is only configured to work with copy number variation data from SNP arrays.
